Modular personal systems hub for structured task, knowledge, and data management.

Overview

Simplify is a modular dashboard designed to unify structured task tracking, knowledge organisation, and personal data tools under a coherent architectural framework. Built around clarity, extensibility, and long-term maintainability.

Architecture

  • Modular feature separation with clear domain boundaries.
  • Structured local data layer.
  • Extensible component-based interface.
  • Designed for cross-device evolution.
