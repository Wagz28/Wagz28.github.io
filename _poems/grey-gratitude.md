A shiver goes down my spine
I see to what most are blind.
Take my hand, embrace it's hug,
I will whisper silently my heart's true love.

The moon has signalled, time to reflect;
A day has passed, a second gone,
Cherish this minute... _oh no! What's on my phone._
The grass is fertile & green, full of life,
Tickles toes & calms woes yet we fear strife.
The sun & moon stay to play
A valley set before us, we endure prolific exuberance today.

Rich & wanting, dark secrets lay
Pure & unsure, nothing to say!
A lion is hungry, but lacks the seer's foresight
I'll tear through, feast on my prey
A shot heard, let the games begin
Life is good, ВСЁ nothing to lose.
Euphoria begins at pain elapsed
I can no longer put here what I put last!
