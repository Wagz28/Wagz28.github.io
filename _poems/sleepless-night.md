_Praise, Praise, Praise_
I will sing for endless days
In darkened nights
In phantom *goodbyes*
As my heart becomes exposed
Fearing a snow white rose
...will tell me I got too close.

And yet my **LORD** is with me,
**He** comforts and consoles
Softens my heart and tells me **He's** in control.
So _Praise, Praise, Praise_
to the **God** of _endless days_
**His** ways are higher than our ways
They are good and pure
If **He** tells me to hold where it hurts
I won't let go even if it burns

_Carry your cross_, as your flesh is torn
_Trust_, when you cannot see how
_Love_, though demons speak doubt
_Praise, Praise, Praise_, For the **LORD Jesus** is with us, _always_.
