---
title: "When they stop texting"
date: 2026-04-15
summary: "I forget. Help me remember O Lord."
tags: [consolation, contemplation, stillness, peace, awe, christian]
---

When they stop texting
I feel like I've failed
Like I wasn't good to them
Like I was too selfish
Or I didn't listen -
Not a 'good' christian,
Just another typical male

When they stop texting
I feel like I wasn't a good friend
Like I haven't witnessed to Christ
And the good that He brings
Like when I say 'like' too much
Like I've let them down, but worse:
Like _Christ_ didn't come to _mend_

When they stop texting
I forget
I forget what a 'good' christian
Really is for me;
Not a man who's life sings
But a man who knows his sin
Who knows he needs
& the goodness of his Father who brings

When they stop texting
I forget.
I forget to look up -
At the stars and the heavens
And look! A shooting star! 
And look, yet again another!
And on my face a smile now swims

I forget You my Lord
I forget You too much
But You are my refuge
You even bring my forgotten lunch!
You provide and You mend
Comfort comes from Your Spirit
Like the wind, He comes where You send...

In the hearts of the lowly
In the hearts of the meek
Of those downtrodden
Of those who know they weak

You are enthroned and mighty
A judge and a friend
Holy Fire is in Your eyes
And yet You caress, and You tend

When they stop texting
I pray I look again to You
When they stop texting
Help me remember,

Your **Word** _always_ rings **True**

[The Lamb ~ William Blake](https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/43670/the-lamb-56d222765a3e1)
