---
title: "Morning Dew"
date: 2026-02-27
summary: "Worship & Adore for what else is so sure?"
tags: [love, worship, praise, christian, fullness]
---

How could we ever comprehend it
How could one ever describe
I find myself unable to work, to live life
Without breaking into song as Adam anew
Sees his love, His morning precious dew
Water of Life, Bread of Heaven
He is more than it all,
making incomplete even seven
Wonder, Glory and Praise
Forever until the end of days
It is all for His Glory,
Amen, indeed His Name's sake
How can I do anything else
But I do not stop
In my work, my every breath
I _must_ praise the Lord
I _must_, for what else could I do?
He is my evening peace, 
yes again, my morning dew.
