date: 2026-05-19
summary: "How to write against what is not? At least i may try"

The waters flow, for how else could they go?
Crashing seas & stirring storms, sunlit bees buzzing
around flowers - swaying gently in soft breeze....

I have used up what I know, 
and I need some new way to show:
	&emsp; How the _power_ of the waters
flow, and flow, and flow.
Like the pressure on a poorly built damn, 
is to hold my disgust against such waters.
As a swamp staying from clean, a fly staying to its sickly gleem,
yet they cannot prevail,
under clean & fresh veil.

"What a sorry sight to see", names the all-seeing tree,
"Under bark & under flower, I see maggots crawling in swagger!"
"A slimy rudder, an achieving place does it try pretend to be finishing the race."
"_But_" riddles in soft embers gleam, "the roots draw from **that** stream."

Indeed the waters flow, for where else could they go?
Once course is set, predestined it has been
A logic gate to some, yet truth remains to be seen.
Tarry a while and tell me so, but yet water goes on--
Piercing shard of snows. It groans & breaks, 
ring of roses as the old man's chapter closes.

The waters must go: for Life, you cannot slow.
