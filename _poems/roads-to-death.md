A leaking heart, a drowning mind
A soul unrested, in the shadows of night
The rocks turn festering, 
The spikes digesting
Sweet poison of mine,
To imagine crossing a line
Or to dismiss it altogether
    &emsp; and shout to be onside!

Delicate wind, in passing heat
Joys complete and fears relieved
Slaves to freedom,
Tested for predispose:
The paradox of weight,
Admittance of the fall
    &emsp; Sets the sky ever close

Not one, not one
Who hear My call
Given choice and care
Free, for I have paid the fare
    &emsp; Simply cry back,
And find you will be found.

Take care for your ways
Lest you turn astray
Pushing it down
Brings only closer ground
    &emsp; and grinded to be nigh.

Pretend not
To be free of shadows of night
Do not only blame the lack of light
For You have a Father
    &emsp; waiting to be let in

Making you _as light as Light_.
