---
title: "To know not"
date: 2026-04-04
summary: "Your heritage, an abomination"
tags: [christian, lament]
---

The nations your heritage
In abomination made forgotten

Mr fool,
on which stool would you like your food?
Dearest writer,
Have I not left my youth?

A single folly!
Nay, a drink to all!!
See, over there, my dearest, so tall!!!!

It is unwise to enchant,
To get caught up with the rest of the runts.
Instead, consider, think a while,
Have tea, or perhaps ponder it over dinner...

Ha! This guyyyy
Take a break!
You'll worry over your limit
And for a nothink's sake?

No. Let us study,
And see what we may be found,
As long as I do not have to better that weather.

And so it went on...
No real tale, just an joking old sail
Flapping in a breeze, a softness, a sigh
A sneeze!! Quick!, take to board,
A life to live, a life to hoard!!!
One time, gotta make a mine.
Tell you who it ain't, ain't none but mine.
Mine! _Mine_, mine?
Oh deary dearest, if you don't mind...
What exactly is the, well, current _time_?
I set the compass you see,
But well, oh, well; nevermind.

[Jeremiah 2](https://www.bible.com/bible/59/JER.2)
