---
title: "Trace the roots"
date: 2026-05-23
summary: "Where eft they go I no longer know"
tags: [wonder, gratitude, christian]
---

Trace the roots
Far and wide
In the poems
Of the night:

Thin and spindly
Where eft they go
Is a question
I no longer know

Flowers in timber
Long leafed they cinder
Beautiful and bright
Never dead; in such Life:

See such leaf
Trusting in what runs deep
Let the Gardner work
And wonder at His sorrowful glee
