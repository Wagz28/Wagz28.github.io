Listen
See
Physically feel Life's energy
And let your heart be free...

Let its power overcome you,
Let is hold you tight.
Be still
Be quiet
You will be a light

We're meant to be together
But where does that leave 'I'...
A very useful letter
The mighty busy '_I_' lets out a long,
no, a short, sharp and quick sigh

All these words, just be shy
retreat into the world,
don't let it pass you by.
Let this be a time of peace
As you read each word piece by piece,
Now slow... down ... and whisper softly
_It's alright_ ... _it's okay_ ... **breathe**.
