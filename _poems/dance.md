To dance, to fly, to be.
Hear the bow stretch, but what did you see?
Shadows play, a choir sings,
A broken clock or a beating heart?
Crashing waves, a new dawn brings
A fresh light, warm from the start.

Whispers silent, beaming smiles
A faulty leg, yet I'd walk for miles.
A curious cat indeed, the most curious cat of all.
Could I sing melody with forever, and dance the eternal ball.
But that is not for today, if even for any I really could not say.
Yes, that's it. A _Dance_, a dance indeed.
That is why I like you мы милий.

But really, truly?... Let us simply wait and see.
Tears true and clear
His voice I hear
My part is due
The thing to which I was born to do.
Love, Beauty, Pain, Life. To give without expectation. **To serve.** Goodnight.
