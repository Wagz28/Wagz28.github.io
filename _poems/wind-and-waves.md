*Winds come, waves crash*
*In Your Love we will always hold fast*
Blessings and glory and honour
Be unto Him who sits in the heavenly places
Whose throne is established forever and ever
And whose people are never lost
For the good shepherd has laid down His life
And so in the depths of our strife
We _cry_ Holy, most Worthy is our Loving Groom
The Love of our hearts in whom we are One
One in body to be flesh and One in Spirit to live afresh
The softness of His Spirit
The closeness of His presence
Like wind is the Spirit
Like a soft breeze upon my heart is His Love
Like a hurricane is His compassion
Like gale is His forgiveness
For His words to the repentant sinner
'Neither do I judge you, go and sin no more'
For faith in Him rebuilds us clean
Crucifixion to His cross _is_ to be free
Submission to His will
Is what it is to rebuild
Good works purified in fire
Upon the foundation of His Love.

Thank You Jesus, thank You.
_Come_ Jesus, Come.

_19/04/26_
