_25/12/25_
A soundless whisper
Swaying in the aroma's of **God's** delight
A single flame
Seeping into deadened night.

Washed souls
Washed clean in words of eternal life
Oh my **LORD**, my **God**, my _loving_ **Father**
As I stretch my mind over savanna's shimmering in strife, glistening green heights and puffy plumes of crystals dancing in white...
I can but bow in wonderful, overwhelming fright
Praise You, Praise You, Praise You
If I didn't, even the dust would cry of Your unsearchable _might_.

Resting, breathing in Hands that hold the foundations of a universe,
The power of the elements silence themselves before Your command,
As You limit Yourself to the body of mortal man,
Lovingly laying upon Yourself the weight of humanity's curse...but wait a moment, we a skipped an important verse;

A power beyond our universe,
wrapped in flesh and bone.
The infinite in a single cell.
A single flame,
Lit to guide us Home.

Jesus, we adore You. Thank you.
