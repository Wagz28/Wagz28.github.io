Don't you find it funny,
Don't you find it sad.
It takes perspective to see what is truly bad.
But perspective comes with time,
Some do not have time to found out what it means to be truly just fine.
A common word, an even commoner lie that no longer has a line.

See it there, being used on the stair,
Flowing elegantly from the lips of a man laid bare.
An empty smile but do they really care.
Broken from illusion, broken from a spell,
Look around to see the millennia who have surely fell.
It should not have been taken, it was not theirs to sell.

When shall this be fear...Judgement day comes at the sound of a bell.
A beautiful creation, a place for one to truly be.
A web to express creativity & one's own identity.

Leaf through the pages and take what is not yours...
I see your deceit, I see your terrible claws.
But should I really dare, should I even care.
I forgive your deceit for I must become unchained & unaware.
I shun your ugly smile for I can go anywhere!
