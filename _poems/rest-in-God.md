Rest in God
In times of trouble, in times of plenty
In times of sadness, in times becoming weary
He does not forsake, 
He will make sure you will never break.
He loves you as a Father to his child
You are His daughter, you are His Son
Take it not for granted, He is your peace in the wild
He is more than a dependence, He is the source
He is the light by which you place your feet,
The force that holds you to the ground,
The force that holds planets to stars, and stars to galaxies beyond
He is, _I am_
He is, and was, and is to come!
Praise Him all ye nations,
Adore Him all you sons and daughters.
Let the Elders cry, Holy and most worthy is His Son, the Lamb who was slain, who's blood brings Life Eternal, who washes death from our spirit and brings His own into our hearts.
Praise Him, Love Him, and in Him...Truly Rest.
