title: "Dreams"
date: 2011-06-03

What dreams have you?
What dreams do I?
Israel's curse, Israel's blessing
Dreams are a funny thing

We get episodic cuts of fuller stories
Weaved together without order
Fragments of never forgotten memories
Passing from murder to murder

Abounding in Grace whilst sleeping in sin
New life in Christ and yet I forget when I'm locked in
All it takes is a Word,
yet taken away by the horror and fray
The twisted delights and temptations coloured bright
In days I would never spend such ways
Yet watch as a hands make do of another's plan
A body of atrocity and any's voracity

But with this I paint the wrong ordeal,
It's not that I indulge or take part
It can only be passing, it misses the feel
The point is not the content, 
the predators or darkness in day,
It is the sickness of spiritual kind
Like the smell of death
Or the nausea of a road that twists and winds
Not yourself in body, nor mind
Forced to feel the inner workings of another's soul
A place of illusion without coherence or fusion
Fusion with a shape that does not take light
As if darkness could become sickeningly bright.

The Lord is my strength, 
I need not know what.
But instead I wake to beautiful new day,
Reality strong and real
Breath that fills your whole body with zeal
Challenge and beauty,
Responsibility and order,
Sits sweet though hard to be
Let all the earth, let the elders shout
For the Lord is coming, coming, coming...
Coming to, for once and for all,
From darkness bring out
