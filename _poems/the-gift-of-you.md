title: "The Gift of You"
date: 2026-05-11
summary: "As if nothing"
tags: [christian, love, submission]
---

Engulf me in Your fire
Wrap me in Your light
Let Your waters cool my flames
And sink peace deep, like dead of night.

As if stars proclaimed Your glory
And pollution - our part in the story.
As if uncertain constants longed to obey Your law
And all creation is growning for the fulness of its source
As if nothing, because it is.

So let us praise Him too,
For You gave us back what we threw away:
You gave us back Life.
You gave us back... 

_You_
