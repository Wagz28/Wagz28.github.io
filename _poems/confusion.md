Chopped like a tree, used for timber
Lay myself down, shelter for their winter
Is this not, Christ-like love,
I'm told it's naïve, I'm a slave not free

But what should I do, put myself on mute
Give it all up, and fly off like a dove
To the safe, dry land - but the kingdom's at hand!!
Rest in His love, die in the night
Strength in the morning by the kindness of His might
Another day, another way, 
to know I'm mostly, messing up my race
It's a lovely day, the sun's warming my face
Stop pretending, stop extending,
take a break and stop explaining
But I'm not pretending, not most of the time - well I have limited time and try my best to rhyme,
but my journey ain't the point!
it's a messy way, to praise God's higher ways,
Endless problems, no hope for solution
Christ came in, and restored God's union,
In order that we may once again enjoy communion.
With the One most high, not just a God in the sky, but the divine as a man, this has always been God's plan.
