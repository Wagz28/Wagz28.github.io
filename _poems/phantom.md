---
title: "Phantom"
date: 2026-04-04
summary: "The dripping claws of a tyger, make naught by a Lamb."
tags: [christian, lament, awe]
---

Such terror, such pain
Naked. 
Upon which I try to look again.

Stars of wonder, star of light
Deep darkness in, draws the night
Seeping, weaving, ever appeasing
Both loom, but only one is bright.

Song birds sing! Practice in your sleep
And leave to be alone, lost and broken for
A sheep without a keep;
The shepherd
Hanging

On a blood stained tree.

Burning coals are scattered,
Heavy wings batter:
At the east gate, fours of fours
Lion, human, eagle and cherub.
Spirit filled body, works filled faith
Yet on hearing was accredited
That righteousness we proclaim!
Who sigh and groan
Over what it is [to know not](https://www.benwagstaff.com/s/poemLink/poetry/to-know-not/)
But for the goodness of The Name
Each rejoices in each own's lot.
For exceedingly so,
O exceedingly so
That passion of Christ:
Suffient, when not forgot.
