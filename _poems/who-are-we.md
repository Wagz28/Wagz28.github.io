---
title: "Who are we?"
date: 2026-04-12
summary: "Not now, but then; memory of a start, not seeing the end. Walking in faith, whatever God sends."
tags: [contemplation, trust, submission, wistful, christian]
---

I do not know.
I cannot see.
For how can one tell,
What is hidden and unseen.

God is our Light.
He entered our darkness.
Yet shine the Light inside...
And what do you see?

A single flame
Dancing in my mind
On fire, ablaze!
A joy to each day:
Again a thought for each night,
A wonder to my soul
That God has made us
Each in His own mold

And as I draw closer
That fire ebbs higher
I ask if I should stay
He tells me it's okay

I have held where He told me
I have carried what I could
I have left all at His feet
And asked for Him if I should

I do not know the way
Only I have the willingness to pay

He goes before and behind me
I plead that the fire stay hot;
And purify what I'm made to be

Yet does anyone know what they ask?
A drop of water in the rain
Now a stranger to my brain
Yet why will it not fall
And melt away from its call

One thing I have learnt
To stay and wait
Wait within a fray
Wait within a breezeless may
And see...
It is God who will decide.
And be...
For it is in God I reside.

His image, for _in Christ_ we are alive.
