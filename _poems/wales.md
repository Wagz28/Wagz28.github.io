The soft breeze bristled through her hair
Hugging the red silhouette lair.
People chattered & the bugs chittered,
But behind all that, if you really listen,
Behind the woofs of distant dogs,
Behind the sweet songs of the merry morning.

Peace.

Its sound eludes many,
& its dance is in the dark alley.
In the little holes and burrows,
In the Welsh countryside furrows...
