_Glorify Your Name, let me fade as dust in wind_
_Only let me see Your glory shine & shine_
_That every knee will bow, even those who fade as i_
_And every tongue **confess** to proclaim, that **Jesus** is **LORD** & **King**_

Satan's circus - a colourful array, a lifetime [week of stay](benwagstaff.com/poetry/week-of-stay)
A maze if you will; 
	&emsp; extra points taking a pill
The prize - god.
	&emsp; To find or, better, to unwind
An eternal comedy, the prince's laboratory

Best not look up or down, but even not in that you will be found
The first - distraction, 
	&emsp; whilst when you can mix in super-abstraction-fractioned
Whenst once thy desperately lie, 
	&emsp; in achievement you find the bottom of the pit nigh
What an exuberant crown for the centre is 'to-die-for',
ah! exquisite, truly what else is there to dine for.

	&emsp; _A house of twisted mirrors_
"Strawberry cyanide Madam", a bowing servant reveres, 
"_finest_ of our _newest_ batch!"
Her royalty strides forward but — 
"Sip quietly", He calls, 
"for thinking on that may tear such a dress, _instead_ turn to address"
The scotsman strides forward
"Heh, these are questions we canni' answer, so we know."
"Oh do write that 'up for me, I can't think on it now",
our English-dun says,
"Effective Immediate" another calls forth.

Turn the page.

A light strikes fine, a fight like mine.
A superstition to equate, for a miracle why wait.
The smartest move is to quit; A fair disagreement!
Yet consider, the wind drives crashing ways on beach-en shore
A spectacle, a painting, looking with the storm
To laugh, to fly, to be - a wonder split within reality
The race, a definitive place, let all yours be free

Then also bow for a fee, _since_ You have _such_ humility
