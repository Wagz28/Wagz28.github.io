---
title: "Stop and Watch"
date: 2026-03-28
summary: "Waist deep in the waters; stop. As the sun rises, watch"
tags: [wonder, awe, fullness, christian]
---

Stop.
As your feet are washed clean
As all in all reveals to the humble & meek
Feel its measure, see the change
Know the purity of its Name
Trace the roots north of here,
Again stop:
When you appreciate what is truly dear.

Such power in the going.
Such as _what was_ can never be the same.
Tell me what you see,
What you know is to be set free
And wonder ... truly wonder, how it is to be

Such presence
Such presence that in abundance flows
What _will_ be.
Stop, and know.
True transformation follows where such abundance must go.

Another measure,
Feel the force behind
From whence such it came,
From who is to whom it is to find
Surely we see it, such as it flows today
Winding, finding
Every child needing minding
In ways unknown, 
From mysteries untold
We step deeper and deeper
Into transformation we become keeper's
Not to hold, 
but keeper's of fire to burn bold
Not only hearers, but fearers
Of the goodness of Him from old

So we walk in discomfort
Taking the burden from those who know not how
To lay it at the feet of Him to whom we bow
And flowing, flowing, this northern river covers
Dry ground in saturation & encapsulation
Of what previously did not
Into creatures many and food of all kind
Fruit for nourishment and leaves of healing
For him who meditates and delight's in His life

For it is truly He who comes
As that which goes and goes
As wind or as a river flows
Rising, comprising many into One,
One in wind, as a current of Will
Redeeming as we bow to new direction
Into Him who takes us beyond
Obselete but good, its dryness gone
Beyond what was to new command
New life in a river taking us to higher land.
And in it we stand...

So stop.
And watch.

Waist deep in life everlasting
For the wedding to end all fasting.
Watch.
As the sun burns bright
Not just light
Holiness cleansing, a fire ever raging
For what is of Him, for what Law defines
For what is accredited, for the end of time
Worthy of more than majesty & splendour
Yet such poor words have I
A recurrence as words cannot satisfy.
Truth.
As clear as can be yet never to be seen
True?
Instead we do see,
A Man who has been
Now here and near,
for Truth lies in the Word,
Not words from I,
So instead truly stop and watch,
For we do not know His time.
Watch.
As water flows from His side
As the Son begins to rise.
Watch.

And perhaps only for a moment
but yet in order to not forget,
Eternity is set in the moment you share
As the water ebbs over
And a new heart first knows care

Such difficulty and struggle exist
Within congregation only looking beyond mist
To new heavens and earth, and ways of the first
Lead on as we stumble,
Lead on till we are humble.

Stop.
And watch

Till your heart is full.
Renewed once again to be poured out,
From the river of the side of One,
Who died on an altar,
Accursed,
Atoning,
For all.

So stop
And follow His call
