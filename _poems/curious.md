So simple, so curious
Why we take life so serious.
It's not a game but we must play, must be okay
but why play this way?

Have you watched a snail
It's so slow, so frail
Yet it stubbornly makes trail
We're the same, can you tell

Nature restores, it's the gift of our Lord
Sit and watch like a proud parent
Each part, so frail
And the strongest - the slowest
The great trees watch time
indeed ... the last shall be first
