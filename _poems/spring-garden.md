Winter's sun in the alpine garden
Children cackle and gleefully play,
the sun warms my face - it's such a beautiful day
You beautiful stars, you beautiful moon, 
yet when the sun comes out my heart does too.
It dances and plays in the beautiful rays,
It shouts for _**God**, Hosanna! Be Praised!_
The furnaced rocks, the weathered wood,
Energy's first form no longer dons its hood.
Spring is coming, Summer is nigh!
I could but barely help, produce a long peaceful sigh.

With velvet-like wind and earth-made aroma,
With the year in adolescence, a new shape takes over
The sculptures of winter shudder and crack
They stretch their legs, smile and yawn... there is nothing they lack
The **Lord** has provided, **His Word** rings _True_
Their slumber ends, they have slept it through
Uttrykker livet og når himmelen faller ned
The preparation has begun,
The bride is to be wed.
