---
title: "Bely"
date: 2026-05-23
summary: "and look ... again the moon calls from me"
tags: [playful, christian, ]
---


Has thou a Spirit of the sea
Has thou the best of all canst see
	&emsp; What art of you
	&emsp; What cries such sigh
Hast thou the tides of draught or breeze?
Hast thou only the moon for relief?

Let me be plain, for I am _not_ here to disdain
_Simple_ curiosity, abounding wonder
The glory of His creation - cast glory asunder
For whenst waiting on death row,
I must concurrently, go

To street & pebble, in-dyed
with trace of race's end, for which I cannot bely
In danger i be to mix up with THY.
