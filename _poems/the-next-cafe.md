Things must die,
That is the lesson of literature...
Things must die.
Each chapter leads to the next,
Each book closes whether or not it happens to have been the best,
Nothing radical,
This poem is not a fest.
It's very plain, cold and grounded:
Things must simply die.
Use 'em and leave 'em
You have no right to more anyways
